DELCO!

Delaware County, PA borders Philadelphia, and is named after the Delaware River. It also borders Delaware. (Yay, tax-free shopping!) Delco takes up 191 square miles and 600,000 people call Delco home. There are 13 colleges and universities located in Delco. These include the selective Bryn Mawr, Haverford, Rosemont, Villanova, and Swarthmore College. Subaru Stadium was built in Chester, PA in 2010, and is the home of the Philadelphia Union soccer team.
