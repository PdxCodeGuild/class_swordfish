Biography of the Greatest Quarterback to have ever played the game: His Excellency - Thomas Patrick Edward Brady

Thomas Patrick Edward Brady was born on August 3rd, 1977 at San Mateo, California. Tom played college football at the University of Michigan - the most winningest football program in college history and the superior team in The Game. Tom led Michigan to a Big Ten Champtionship and subsequently was the 199th pick in the 2000 NFL Draft by the New England Patriots. With a heavy chip on his shoulder, Tom led New England to SIX Super Bowl victories and has won FIVE Super Bowl MVPs. Tom now plays for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers where he has won an addtional Super Bowl.
