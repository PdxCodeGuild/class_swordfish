Seoul, South Korea

Seoul, formally Sŏul-t’ŭkpyŏlsi (“Special City of Seoul”)

It is the largest metropolis city and the capital of South Korea.

It is located on the Han River in the northwestern part of the country.

As of 2020 Seoul has a population of 9.9 million people.

In 2014 Seoul was named the world's 4th largest metropolitan economy.
