BROKEN TOP

Broken Top is a dormant volcano named for its appearance- the top of the mountain was ground away by glaciers. The mountain lies in the cascades mountain range in Oregon, close to the city of Bend, OR. The mountain is a popular climbing destination with a summit of 9,177 ft. There are also beautiful trails surrounding the mountain, making it a popular hiking destination as well. The mountain is very close to the three sisters, which are also great places to hike and climb.
