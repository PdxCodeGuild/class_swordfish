Robert Edwin House - more commonly known as Mr. House - is a mechanical mastermind that resides at the Lucky 38 Casino. After studying at the Commonwealth Institute of Technology, and at the age of 22, he would go on to found RobCo Industries. Robco would go on to become one of the most profitable corporations in the world. By the time he was 30, he was a billionare 30 times over. However, in the coming years, Mr. House saw the impedning doom of worldwide nuclear annihalation. He began to work on a defense system to protect him, and the Lucky 38 hotel. Mr. House had his body hooked up an advanced life support machine, while his mind was uploaded into his informational network via a super-computer. On October 23rd, 2077 The Great War began, but Mr. House would live to see the world after war. Aptly labeled a technical mastermind and world-class business man, Mr. House now mysteriously lives among those in the Mojave Wasteland in the year 2281.
