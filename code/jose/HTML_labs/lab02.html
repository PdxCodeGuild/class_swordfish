Portland: a Foodie's Paradise

Did you know that Portland was just named America's top food city? If not, well here are some places you should check out that might help you understand why!

1. YUI, a fantastic Thai restaurant located in Northeast. Has great cocktails too!
2. Pope House, a very homey Bourbon lounge located in Northwest. Yummy cocktails and pretty great hushpuppies.
3. Loyal Legion, a great place in Southeast to find upwards of a 100 taps of great beer! Don't forget to get one of their delicious smash burgers.
4. Double Dragon, another Southeast spot with delicious fusion Vietnamese food where you can find a vietnamese styled ramen!
5. Matt's BBQ Food Cart located in Northeast where their amazing BBQ recently made an apperance on a Netflix food documentary, Somebody Feed Phil.
6. Bible Club, a shnazzy cocktail lounge located in Southeast featuring some very decadent cocktails and bites
Don't worry, these are only to get you started. Throw a stone anywhere in Portland and you are sure to find even more delicious treats and drinks where it may land! Cheers!
City of Portland, 2022
